    Universal High COVID-19 update -

    The global health crisis incited by COVID-19 has forced everyone to home confinement and social distancing. As educators, we see this situation not only as an opportunity to impart valuable life lessons to our students but also as our moral responsibility to stand by the parents in nurturing our children while they are at home.
